Make It Reel - 2013

Make It Reel represented a turning point for BMR. The tunes were born out of a weekend writing session at a lake house in Webster, IN in the dead of winter. It was the first time I got to make a record to analog tape, which still sounds the best to my ear. It was recorded by my friend Alex Kercheval at the now defunct Queensize Studios in Indianapolis, IN.

Variety Age - 2018

Variety Age was recorded at Postal Recording by Alex Kercheval, and I still think it may be the best sounding record I've been on. Nick Vote is one of my favorite songwriters and I'm lucky to help him bring life to his tunes. His work on this record along with the work of the band is the most cohesive and self-assured to date.

Tripl3 Dipp3d - 2018

Trippl3 Dipp3d is one of my darkhorse all-time favorites Ive been a part of. Just read the titles of the songs and you'll get a sense of how fun it was to make this record. Gas Station Art had the best unspoken mantra that whatever made all of us crack up is what would go on the record. Secretly, I probably listen to this record the most of everything I've done still precisely because of how absurd it is.
